Welcome to RabDB2

RabDB is a database created to explore the universe of Rabs and Rab subfamilies predicted by the Rabifier classification pipleline. The Rabifier has predicted thousands of novel Rabs, and assigned putative subfamiiles to each of them. Here you can browse this universe, view evolutionary profiles, and submit your own sequences to the Rabfifier to be classified to a subfamily.

Rabify

Submit a protein sequence to find out if it is a Rab, and if so, to what subfamily it belongs.

Browse and Profile Rabs

Browse the universe of Rabs across 244 species that have been analyzed by the Rabifier and view their evolutionary profiles across all major eukaryotic branches.
