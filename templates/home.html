Welcome to RabDB

RabDB is a database created to explore the universe of the Rab family of small GTPases, key regulators of the Eukaryotic endomembrane system, predicted by the Rabifier classification pipleline in the sequenced eukaryotic genomes. The Rabifier has predicted thousands of Rabs, and assigned putative subfamilies to each of them. Here you can browse this universe, view evolutionary profiles, and submit your own sequences to the Rabifier.

Rabify

Submit a protein sequence to find out if it is a Rab GTPase, and if so, to what subfamily does it belong.

Browse Rabs

Browse the universe of Rabs across 244 species that have been analyzed by the Rabifier and view their evolutionary profiles across all major eukaryotic branches.
